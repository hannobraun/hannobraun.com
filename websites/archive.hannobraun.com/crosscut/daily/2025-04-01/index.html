That's it, I guess! I've said all I have to say about the type system. (Except, I already have notes for a series to recap and clarify one specific aspect of it; but that's not a topic for today.) Next, I think it's time for an update on development! After all, I've been working on the new prototype for more than two months.

To be blunt, the whole thing has been a bit of a pain, exactly as predicted. Having a code database has wide-reaching consequences. The compiler is looking completely different than any previous iteration. The custom structure editor I'm building has been a source of much work; and it's very clear, it will take a long time to get right.

These factors are turning every little problem that I face into a micro-R&D project. There's just so much to figure out. And that has made progress on the actual language very, very slow. But so far, I still believe it's worth it. I knew what I was getting into, after all.
